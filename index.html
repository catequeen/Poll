Quick Poll

A no-server scheduling poll. Anyone in the group can create a poll; everything runs in the browser and responses travel by email.

1 · Create
2 · Respond
3 · Tally

Create a poll

Pick a month, then check/uncheck specific dates. Weekdays are pre-selected; uncheck any date to exclude it.

Share it

Send the link below to your group. Each person opens it, makes their picks, and emails you back a short response code.

Respond

Open the poll link your organizer sent you and this tab fills in automatically. (Or use Create → Preview.)

If the email button doesn't open your mail app, copy the code above and paste it into a reply.

Tally responses

Paste the response codes you received (one per line, or paste whole emails — codes are detected automatically).
